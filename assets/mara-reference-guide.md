# Mara Reference Guide

Use Mara as the consistent human face of Rolling Reno across homepage heroes, About page galleries, and future brand imagery.

## Keep these traits consistent
- Warm, capable, practical presence
- Working in or around the RV, not posed like generic travel lifestyle stock
- Earthy, natural wardrobe and workshop-adjacent props
- RV materials, tools, plans, tape measures, wood, fixtures, or paint visible when possible

## Priority image set to preserve
1. Mara with the RV before renovation
2. Mara in active demolition or teardown
3. Mara planning a build step with sketches or measurements
4. Mara installing or finishing a detail
5. Mara in the completed or nearly completed space

## Usage rules
- Prefer candid, action-oriented shots over polished portraits
- Keep lighting warm and natural
- Avoid swapping in unrelated people or anonymous stock faces
- When generating or commissioning new visuals, match Mara's approved facial identity, hair, age range, and general styling from the chosen reference set
- Reuse this guide whenever new landing pages or social assets are created
